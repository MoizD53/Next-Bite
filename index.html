Welcome to Next-Bite

Turning food waste into affordable meals for students and communities. Together, we can build a hunger-free, waste-free campus.

Learn More

The Problem

Every night, hostel messes and restaurants throw away fresh but unsold food. Meanwhile, students often struggle to find affordable food late at night. This creates both food waste and hunger in our communities.

Our Solution

Smart Platform

Restaurants list surplus food safely before closing hours.

Affordable Meals

Students order meals at 50% lower cost through our app.

Community Delivery

Student volunteers and part-timers deliver food quickly.

Key Benefits

Zero Waste

Reduce food wastage by redirecting leftovers.

Zero Hunger

Provide affordable meals to students in need.

Eco-Friendly

Support a sustainable and socially responsible system.
